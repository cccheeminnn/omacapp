FAQ
Q: What is the use case here?
A: For users who do not have complete addresses to do their mailers. Or anyone who needs to complete a list of incomplete addresses, eg lacking in postal code.
Q: How do I use this app?
A: User should upload a .csv file populated with street names.
example 10, 15, or 25 Singapore street names.
Two things to make sure that they are inside the uploaded file is
the column header should have "address" and the column fields should consist ONLY of the street name.
It does not matter if the "address" column is somewhere else not in the first column.
Q: What do you mean by .csv format file?
A: Excel has two types of .csv file,
*CSV format
CSV UTF-8 format
this app is only compatible with the *CSV format (left), not CSV UTF-8 (right).
Q: Request timeout?
A: As Heroku has a request timeout duration of 30s, for 20 and above searches we recommend having the file sent straight to your email instead.
